Introduction

Blue Eye Boutique

Welcome to Blue Eye Boutique. It is a clothing boutique with trendy brands that only have online shops. This store will allow people to try on and get a feel for clothes that have a big online presence, it saves you the shipping and hassle with possible returns.

Target audience

The target audience is gauged towards gen z and young adults/fresh adults.

The people who will use the site will be the teens interested in shopping here and the parents that are curious about prices. The goal of this audience is to be more informed about the shop, its mission, what they sell, the price range of the products, and what the boutique will be selling.
